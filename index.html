About Me

January 12th, 1990, that's my day.

I spent my early life in Fiuggi, a small town not so far from Rome. It was nice at the end, but I didn't wait so much time when I had the chance to move away from there after high school. Turin was my choice (but I still dream to move to San Francisco bay area), in which I had my degree in Computer Engineering at Polytechnic University of Turin in 2012, and it's here that my passion for Computer Science started growing. Still in Turin, after the bachelor degree I immediately applied for the Master of Engineering but I also participated in an university exchange program in Grenoble to study Computer Security (no, I'm not a kind of Black Hat, I swear!). Back in Turin after one year, I finished my Thesis in "Trustworthiness Marketplaces" and started working for a consulting company.

Yes, of course, consultancy is nice, but from the spring of 2016 I decided to give a boost to my life and, with some friends of mine, we created Fuseelab, our idea of Startup, with some great projects in minds and some others already under construction. During the same period I started working for Google Developer Group Torino, in which I'm following the external relations and taking care of the entrepreneurial aspects.

Apart from technology and Computer Science, my passions are music, cycling, and, of course, networking. Do not hesitate to contact me if you want to collaborate with GDG Torino or Fuseelab!
