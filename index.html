About Me

January 12th, 1990, that's my day.

I spent my early life in Fiuggi, a small town not so far from Rome. It was nice at the end, but I didn't wait so much time when I had the chance to move away from there after high school. Turin was my choice (but I still dream to move to San Francisco bay area), in which I had my degree in Computer Engineering at Polytechnic University of Turin in 2012, and it's here that my passion for Computer Science started growing. Still in Turin, after the bachelor degree I immediately applied for the Master of Engineering but I also participated in an university exchange program in Grenoble to study Computer Security (no, I'm not a kind of Black Hat, I swear!). Back in Turin after one year, I'm concluding my Thesis in "Trustworthiness Marketplaces", in the mean time working in a couple of projects for a consulting company.

Apart from technology and computer science, music is my passion and I really miss those days playing my Tama Swingstar.
